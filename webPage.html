Space Log Tracker

Welcome to the Space Log Tracker! This application allows you to keep track of your space-related activities and logs.

Use the navigation menu to access different sections of the tracker, including:

  • Logs
  • Statistics
  • Settings

Logs

Here you can view and manage your space logs. You can add new logs, edit existing ones, and delete logs that are no longer needed.

Statistics

In this section, you can view various statistics related to your space activities. This includes total time spent in space, number of missions, and more.

Settings

Customize your Space Log Tracker experience by adjusting settings such as notification preferences, display options, and account information.
